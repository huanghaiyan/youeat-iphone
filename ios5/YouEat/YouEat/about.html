YouEat

YouEat on the web www.youeat.org.
YouEat started from the aspiration to have an easy and fast tool to share the passion for the good food.
YouEat wants to be:
  • an easy to use application to create articles on restaurants, wine bars, pizzerias, eat cafe.
  • a place to share food and drink experiences
